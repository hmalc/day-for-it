# Day For It Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026

Day For It is an Australian marine-conditions planning app with full local tide and wave detail currently limited to supported Queensland areas. It does not require an account, does not include advertising, does not use analytics SDKs, and does not track you across apps or websites.

## Information Stored On Your Device

Day For It stores your selected forecast area on your device so the app can reopen to the same area. This may be a built-in Australian area, a manual coordinate you entered, or a current-location coordinate if you chose to use current location.

You can replace this stored preference in Settings by choosing another area, saving another manual location, using current location again, or returning to the Cowley Beach default. Deleting the app removes the stored preference.

## Location

Day For It requests iOS When In Use location permission only when you tap "Use my current coastal location." Location is used to select a supported nearby Australian coastal forecast area, with tide and wave detail where available. The app does not request background location access.

The app does not operate its own server and does not sell or share location data for advertising or analytics. Standard network information, such as your IP address, may be processed by Apple and by the public data services the app contacts to service forecast, observation, warning, tide, and wave requests.

## Network Data Sources

Day For It downloads marine forecast, observation, warning, tide, and wave information from public Australian weather and Queensland open-data services. These requests are used only to provide app functionality.

## Safety

Day For It is for planning only. It is not a navigation aid, emergency alert system, or replacement for official forecasts, warnings, tide tables, local signage, or current conditions.
